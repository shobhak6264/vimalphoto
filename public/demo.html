About Us

Established in Singapore in 2004, Unifab Technologies Pvt Ltd is committed to helping our customers meet their business goals, through better Automation & Communication systems. Unifab’s breadth of experience & expertise in Process Automation, Building Automation, Software Development and Communication Systems, helps relieve our customers’ system design & implementation related pain points. Unifab also leverages this proficiency to support our clients in smoothly deploying IoT (Internet of Technologies) and Industry 4.0 solutions.

Our Mission

Through our emphasis on customer’s satisfaction, we have built an excellent reputation as trusted partner in the Automation Industry. We are a dynamic and aggressive team, comprising of Industry Veteran Directors, Hands-on Engineers and Domain Expert Advisors, all with commitment and drive to focus on our clients’ needs in enhancing and achieving better Systems.
